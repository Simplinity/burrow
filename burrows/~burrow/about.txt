# About Burrow

The internet is bloated. We dug a way out.

Burrow is a plaintext content server inspired by Gopher, built for people who think the web peaked around 1997 but couldn't quite admit it until now.

It serves your words over HTTP, HTTPS, and Gemini. It renders them in beautiful typography. It refuses to execute JavaScript. (One scroll handler got through. It's on probation.)

Every burrow lives in a ~username/ directory, because tildes were the original social network and we will die on this hill.

This burrow is the manual. Each page covers one feature. Read them in order or wander. That's what burrows are for.
