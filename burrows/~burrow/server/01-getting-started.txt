# Getting Started

You need two things: the burrow binary and something to say.

---

Install with one command:

  curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Simplinity/burrow/master/install.sh | bash

This gives you two binaries: burrowd (the server) and burrow (the CLI). The total download is smaller than a typical React component.

Create your first burrow:

  burrow init yourname

This creates a ~yourname/ directory with an about.txt and a phlog/ folder. That's your entire content management system. You're welcome.

Write your first post:

  burrow new "Why I left Medium"

This opens your $EDITOR with a file named 2026-03-22-why-i-left-medium.txt. Write something. Save. Close. It's published.

Start the server:

  burrowd

Visit http://localhost:7070. There's your burrow. There are your words. There's nothing else competing for attention. That's the point.

---

The whole process takes about 90 seconds. That's less time than it takes to accept the cookie banner on most websites.
