# Why Plaintext Matters — Part 2: The Solution

Plaintext is the most durable, portable, and honest format ever invented.

A .txt file from 1985 opens in 2026 without conversion, without a plugin, without a subscription. Try that with a Word document from 2005. Or a Flash animation from 2010. Or a Medium post from a startup that no longer exists.

Plaintext doesn't go out of business. It doesn't get acquired. It doesn't pivot to video.
