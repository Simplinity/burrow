# Why Plaintext Matters — Part 3: The Future

Burrow is built on a bet: that enough people are tired of the extractive web to build something better. Not different for the sake of different. Better in ways that matter.

Faster. Because 5 KB loads in 50ms on any connection.
Durable. Because text files don't break.
Honest. Because there's nothing hiding behind the words.
Yours. Because the files are on your disk, not in someone's database.

---

The future of publishing is the past of publishing, with better typography.
