# On Slowness

The internet moves fast. That's not a feature. That's a symptom.

When everything demands your attention right now, nothing gets your attention fully. We scroll, we skim, we move on. The content is consumed, not read. The words pass through us like water through a sieve.

What if we slowed down? Not as a lifestyle brand. Not as an aesthetic. Just... slower. Read one thing today. Really read it. Let it sit. Come back tomorrow and read something else.

The phlog format helps. No infinite scroll. No "recommended for you." No autoplay. Just words, waiting patiently for someone who cares enough to read them.

Slowness isn't laziness. It's respect — for the writer, for the reader, for the words themselves.
